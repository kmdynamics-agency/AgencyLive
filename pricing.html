FAQ

Pricing Questions

Is ad spend included in the price?
No, ad spend is separate from our management fees. We recommend minimum budgets per plan (listed above) but you control how much you spend on ads. We optimize every rand for maximum ROI.
What's the contract length?
We offer month-to-month flexibility with no long-term contracts required. However, we recommend committing to at least 3 months for best results, as marketing takes time to compound.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply to your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any differences.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EFT, credit/debit cards, and can set up debit orders for your convenience. Invoices are sent on the 1st of each month.
Do you offer refunds?
Due to the nature of our work (content creation, ad setup, strategy development), we don't offer refunds. However, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
What if I need something custom?
We love custom projects! Contact us with your specific needs and we'll create a tailored proposal. Enterprise clients often have unique requirements we're happy to accommodate.
